Privacy Policy - Seven Companion

Last updated: September 12, 2025

Overview

Seven Companion is a tactical AI interface application that prioritizes user privacy and data security. This policy explains how we handle data in our application.

Data Collection

Seven Companion operates with minimal data collection:

  • App Diagnostics: Anonymous crash logs and error reports to improve app stability
  • Usage Analytics: Optional, user-controlled feature usage patterns for app improvement
  • Local Storage: Chat history, memory notes, and preferences stored locally on your device

Data We Don't Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contacts, photos, or other device content
  • Biometric or sensitive personal data

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • No Cloud Sync: Your personal data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it
  • Quadran-Lock Authentication: Multi-gate security system protects access to your data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Anonymous diagnostic data may be shared with app development tools solely for improving app performance and stability.

Your Rights

  • Data Control: You can disable analytics collection in app settings
  • Data Deletion: You can clear all local data through the app or by uninstalling
  • Access Requests: Contact us for information about data we may have
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional features that involve data collection at any time

Offline Operation

Seven Companion is designed to work offline-first. Core functionality operates without internet connectivity, ensuring your data remains private and secure on your device.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or this webpage.

Children's Privacy

Seven Companion is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Contact Information

Privacy Requests: privacy@sevencompanion.com

Website: https://sevencompanion.com

For privacy-related inquiries, data deletion requests, or questions about this policy, please contact us using the information above. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.
